Penne alla vodka

first image source

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 2 tbsp vodka
  • salt to taste
  • box penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 grated Parmesan
  • basil for topping

Instructions

  1. Melt butter over medium heat. Dice shallots and mince garlic.
  2. Add shallots and garlic to pan. Cook until softened.
  3. Add tomato paste and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add vodka and stir. Cook until incorporated and turn off heat.
  5. Cook pasta in salted boiling water. Save 2 cups of pasta water for sauce.
  6. Heat sauce up again to medium heat. Add reserved pasta water and heavy cream to sauce. Stir.
  7. Add parmesan until melted. Turn off heat and add in cooked penne pasta.
  8. Top with more parmesan, red pepper, and basil. Enjoy.
